Keep Them Fresh

Temperature

Keep at -20°C for long-term storage. A standard fridge (2-8°C) is fine for short periods.

Environment

Store away from light and moisture. Keep the vials sealed until you're ready to use them.

Best Practices

Use amber glass if transferring, but our original vials are designed for long-term stability.

Handling Your Shipment

Safety First

Always use gloves and a clean workspace when handling the research materials.

Mixing Guide

Use sterile water or a proper buffer. Mix gently—don't shake or use a vortex.

Lab Disposal

Follow your local lab safety rules for disposal. Keep your research area clean.
